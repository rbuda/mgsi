Dan Nebuda

Dan grew up in West Point, NE. He started learning the glass trade in the mid 70's and would move to Columbus over 25 years ago. He sought out his passion to be a business owner and help people. In 1991, Dan & his family moved to Columbus and he became owner and manager of Midwest Glass Services Inc.

Dan loves being a fan of all Husker sports, is active at his church Peace Lutheran, and most of all spending time with his family. His wife, son, daughter and now grandson keep him plenty busy.
