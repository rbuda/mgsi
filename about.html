Dan Nebuda

Dan grew up in West Point, Nebraska. He has been in the glass business for over 40 years, the last 25 years as owner of Midwest Glass Service, Inc. He and his wife Mona, son Ryan, and daughter Katie, moved to Columbus Nebraska in December of 1990, and began to manage Midwest Glass Service Inc. in January of 1991.


Dan loves being a fan of all Husker sports, is a member of Peace Lutheran Church, and most of all, spending time with his family, including a young grandson, Elliot.

Mike Brandt

Mike Brandt was the first employee that Dan hired in 1991. He is a native of Columbus, Nebraska. He has worked at Midwest Glass for over 25 years. Mike started working at Midwest Glass shortly after graduating from high school. His pride in workmanship and quality of work shows in the work he performs.


Mike and his wife, Amy, have 3 daughters and 1 son. They are members of St Bonaventure Church, and their children attend St Bonaventure School and Scotus Central Catholic School. Mike is also a Husker fan, and spends a lot of time helping with the sporting activities of his children.
